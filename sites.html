How to solve

Notation

The basic moves for a 3x3 are as follows:

U

D

R

L

F

B

U'

D'

R'

L'

F'

B'

Each letter indicates a turn of that face 90 degrees clockwise

An apostrophe means to reverse the move (turn counterclockwise).

A 2 just means do the move twice

White Cross

Find an edge with white on one side, hold the cube with white on top and the other color on the edge piece in front
use D and F moves to bring the edge into the top front, if the colors don't line up, do F' L D L' F2 to flip the piece
Repeat with all 4 white edges

White Corners

*White face is on the bottom*

Find a corner with a white side, use U moves to place the corner above where it should go.

If the white side is on the right, turn the cube so the corner is on the right and use R U R' U'
If the white side is on the left, turn the cube so the corner is on the left and use L' U' L U

If the white side is on top, you can pick either set of moves and do it 3 times
