Solving

Notation Each letter indicates a turn of that face 90 degrees clockwise An apostrophe means to reverse the move (turn counterclockwise). A 2 just means do the move twice White Cross Find an edge with white on one side, hold the cube with white on top and the other color on the edge piece in front use D and F moves to bring the edge into the top front, if the colors don't line up, do F' L D L' F2 to flip the piece Repeat with all 4 white edges White Corners *White face is on the bottom* Find a corner with a white side, use U moves to place the corner above where it should go. If the white side is on the right, turn the cube so the corner is on the right and use R U R' U' If the white side is on the left, turn the cube so the corner is on the left and use L' U' L U If the white side is on top, you can pick either set of moves and do it 3 times Edges *White face is on the bottom* Find an edge that dosn't have yellow, line it up with the face that is on the side If the top side is the color on the right, do U R U R' U' F' U' F If the top side is the color on the left, do U' L' U' L U F U F' Yellow Cross *White face is on the bottom* If you have all 4 yellow edges on top, skip to the next step If the yellow edges on top form a V, put the V in the top left corner and do the algorithm twice If the yellow edges on top form a line, put the line right-left and do the algorithm F R U R' U' F' Yellow Corners *White face is on the bottom* The goal of this step is to find a fishy :) you should have a yellow X on top and any number ofcorners faceing up if only 1 corner is up, put that corner on the front left and do the algorithm in any other circumstance, find the edge with the least yellow and do the algorithm with that side in front R U R' U R U2 R' Position Yellow Corners *White face is on the bottom* Now the corners, look at all 4 sides and try to find a side with matching corners if you find one, put it on the left face and do the algorithm B' R B' L2 B R' B' L2 B2 Position Yellow Edges *White face is on the bottom* The last step is to put the edges into place, these 2 algorithms rotate the edges on the front, right, and left faces Clockwise F2 U L R' F2 L' R U F2 Counter-Clockwise F2 U' L R' F2 L' R U' F2
